Welcome to Our Deb_Soc Website

DEB SOC is our debate society where students and anyone interested can come together to talk, debate, and improve their communication skills. It’s a place to share ideas, discuss different views, and learn how to express thoughts confidently. Through debates and conversations, we help people overcome stage fright, become more confident speakers, and develop skills that are super useful in school and beyond. Join us in this fun and enriching experience where everyone’s opinions are valued!
